19.1 Responsive video

You may need to embed video from other third party websites into your website. To keep the video responsive it needs to sit in it's own <div>. Just replace the link with your own.

<div class="flex-video">
   <iframe src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/p41FZX0MOtk?rel=0" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
</div>
